Guess the number in the box (out of 100)

  1. The guess is any number between 1 and 100, making it almost impossible to win the game, especially twice in a row
  2. The player has just three guesses under his belt to tell what number is there.
  3. After the first two tries, the player is told he is wrong if he is, and given a third try to guess the number. if he is still wrong, he loses the round and 0 is added to his scoreboard, with 1 added to the number of games he's played.
  4. After the player fails a round, the hidden number for that round is shown
  5. The 'Play Game' button displays the game section in favour of the rules section. The 'See Rules' button does the opposite.
  6. On inputting any guess, the user must click on the 'Check' button to see whether they are correct.
  7. The reset button is used to reboot the game, killing everything that has previously been recorded up to that time.
  8. The 'Quit Game' button removes the game section from display. The 'Quit Rules' button removes the rules section from display.
    Activating one section automatically disables the other.
  9. Goodluck to the player!
